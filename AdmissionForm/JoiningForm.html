Legal Terms and Conditions

1. Eligibility: Students of all ages and skill levels are welcome. For students under 18, parental consent is required.

2. Registration: Admission is confirmed only upon completion of the registration form and payment of applicable fees.

3. Fees: All fees must be paid in advance. Refunds are not provided for missed classes.

4. Attendance: Regular attendance is expected. Please inform the studio in advance of any absences.

5. Conduct: Students are expected to behave respectfully towards instructors and fellow students. Disruptive behavior may result in dismissal from the program.

6. Dress Code: Appropriate dance attire and footwear are required for all classes. Details will be provided upon registration.

7. Health and Safety: Students must disclose any medical conditions or injuries that may affect their participation. The studio reserves the right to request a doctor's note.

8. Photography/Videography: The studio may use photographs or videos of students for promotional purposes. By signing up, you grant permission for such use.

9. Termination: The studio reserves the right to terminate a student's enrollment for breach of these terms and conditions or any other reason deemed necessary.
